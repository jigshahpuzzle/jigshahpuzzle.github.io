At My Startup



My team and I founded Finalyze in 2015 to re-invent the way investment opportunities are researched. We built two financial research systems and scaled them to thousands of users. Try them out below:


  • Finalyze Screener provides fundamental financial data on S&P 500 companies and is available for free to the public. Our goal with Finalyze Screener was to condense troves of financial data into concise investment insights for everyday investors. As a result, our team trained a set of neural networks to summarize SEC Filings, Earnings Call Transcripts, and Financial Statements into the most important data points, automatically compiling them into research reports.



  • Finalyze Premium is a business intelligence platform that brings alternative datasets into the traditional investment analysts' workflow, empowering them to go from hypothesis to data-driven investment insight in a matter of minutes. The platform features search functionality over millions of datasets, drag-and-drop machine learning tools, and modern data visualization capabilities. If you are a fund manager interested in how the Finalyze solution can help you, sign up for a product demo.
